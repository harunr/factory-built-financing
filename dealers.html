HOW IT WORKS

Are you looking for a way to simplify the process of completing and submitting loan applications for your potential manufactured home buyers? Factory Built Financing is a free service that will help you speed up and improve this process. Here’s how it works.

  1. You create a free account for manufactured home dealers on FactoryBuiltFinancing.com.
  2. You send your potential home buyers to FactoryBuiltFinancing.com to complete their loan applications.
  3. Buyers will search for your dealership by phone number and will identify you as the dealer they are working with before starting their application.
  4. When a new loan application is completed, a PDF version of the loan application will be sent to your portal, and you will receive an email notice letting you know it has arrived.
  5. You will scan and upload the buyer’s lender permission forms.
  6. You will select the lenders from your list of saved lenders to email the loan application and permission forms to and click submit.

Please Note :You can also print and/or download completed loan applications and then manually fax or email them to the lender(s) of your choice.
